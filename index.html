The Idea

The outdoors shouldn't require a spreadsheet. Recreation.gov, state parks, private sites, BLM land — each with its own system, its own calendar, its own friction. We built Campgrounder to fix that.

One honest place to browse, decide, and go. No ads. No dark patterns. No algorithm nudging you somewhere you don't want to be. Just trees, trails, and the cleanest path between you and them.
